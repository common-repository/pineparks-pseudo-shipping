Pineparks Pseudo Shipping: Preview shipping methods, reduce checkout confusion and lower checkout abandonment.

== Description ==

Say goodbye to the customers frustration because of hidden shipping methods during checkout. The Pineparks Pseudo Shipping plugin for Woocommerce allows you to add temporary "pseudo" shipping methods that are immediately visible during the checkout process. No more waiting for customers to enter their address before seeing all available shipping methods - improve their experience and reduce checkout confusion.

Streamline your checkout with Pineparks Pseudo Shipping. 
This small but powerful plugin makes it easy to add temporary shipping methods to your checkout process. Your customers will have a clear preview of available shipping methods before entering their address, making the checkout experience faster and more accurate. With preview shipping methods, you reduce checkout confusion and lower checkout abandonment. 
Say goodbye to guesswork and hello to a seamless checkout experience with Pineparks Pseudo Shipping.

After installing and activation the plugin, you will be able to add unlimited amount of custom shipping methods. This methods will appear in checkout as other shipping methods do, but before the customer inputs or select the city of destination.


= Features =

- Preview shipping options: Allows customers to preview available shipping methods before entering their address.
- Temporary shipping methods: Adds temporary shipping methods to the checkout process for a more efficient experience.
- Streamlined checkout: Simplifies the checkout process and reduces confusion for customers.
- Reduced purchase abandonment: Lower purchase abandonment by providing clear and accurate information during the checkout process.

== Installation ==

1. Please make sure that you installed WooCommerce
2. Go to plugins in your dashboard and select "Add New"
3. Search for "Pineparks Pseudo Shipping for Woocommerce", Install & Activate it
4. Now when you create/edit a product you can choose product type is "Smart Bundle", then add products as you want
5. Almost done! click Save and see the result
